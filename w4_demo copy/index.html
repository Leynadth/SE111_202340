ABOUT The Batman

The new Batman movie is here to blow our minds and ignite our Gotham-loving hearts. This flick isn't just another run-of-the-mill superhero saga; it's a pulse-pounding, dark and gritty ride into the shadows of Bruce Wayne's soul. Our brooding hero is back in action, prowling the neon-lit streets of a city overrun by a fresh breed of baddies. With a lead actor who totally nails the cowl, we're in for an adrenaline-fueled joyride through a Gotham that's as moody and mysterious as ever. From bone-chilling showdowns with classic foes to jaw-dropping visuals that'll have us questioning reality, this movie honors the Bat's legacy while giving us a front-row seat to an all-new, all-awesome chapter in his saga. Time to suit up and get ready for a Bat-tastic cinematic experience!

Release date: March 4, 2022 (USA)

Director: Matt Reeves

Cinematography: Greig Fraser

Nominations: Academy Award for Best Visual Effects, MORE
